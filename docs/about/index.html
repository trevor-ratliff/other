Who is Trevor Ratliff?

He's a programmer, photographer, 3-D animator and crafter extrodinaire.   He has been programming professionally for 15 years in all sorts of areas, such as web apps, console programs, desktop appliactions and more.   He picked up videography and photography in high school and has been improving his eye ever since. There was a natural progression to 3-D animation from his love of videography and computers and the skill sets in both compliment each other wonderfully. In the physical world he has been crafting since he was 5, starting with Origami.
